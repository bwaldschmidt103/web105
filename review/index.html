IP Man

I am reviewing the movie IP Man. It was released in 2008. The story is about Bruce Lee’s master of Kung Fu. The story takes place between the 1930s and 1940s in a city known as Foshan, China.

Master of Kung Fu

IP man is a wealthy martial artist and hush hush teacher, who was forced to leave China during the Sino-Japanese War. The Japanese invaded his city and started a war with his country. His people were forced into slavery and treated with disrespect.
